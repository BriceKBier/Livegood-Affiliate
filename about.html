About the founder

Hi, I’m Brice Bier.

I created UCanWorkFromHome to help adults 50+ find practical ideas for living healthier, earning with greater flexibility and enjoying the next chapter of life.

Myrtle Beach, South Carolina

Why I created this site

After many years in business and later becoming a licensed Realtor in South Carolina, I became interested in the possibilities of building income online. I liked the flexibility, the low overhead and the fact that a person could continue learning and building at any age.

At the same time, healthy living became more important to me. Like many adults, I wanted clearer information about nutrition, supplements, exercise and the many wellness products competing for our attention.

UCanWorkFromHome brings those interests together.

What I write about

I publish practical articles about work-from-home opportunities, affiliate marketing, retirement-friendly income ideas, healthy aging, nutrition and wellness products.

I do not claim to have every answer. My role is to research, compare, explain and share what I learn in a straightforward way so readers can make their own informed decisions.

My approach

I believe useful information should be understandable, realistic and honest about limitations. That means discussing costs and drawbacks—not just benefits—and avoiding guarantees about income or health outcomes.

Some articles contain affiliate or referral links. When they do, I may earn compensation at no added cost to the reader. That relationship never changes my responsibility to be clear about what a product or opportunity can and cannot do.

“My goal is to help readers make smarter decisions about their health, time and money—without pressure.”

Welcome

Whether you are looking for a flexible way to earn extra income, exploring better wellness habits or simply beginning a new chapter, I hope this site gives you useful ideas and a sensible place to start.

— Brice Bier

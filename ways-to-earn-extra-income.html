A word of caution

Treat rewards sites as bonus money

  • Never count on a specific monthly income.
  • Do not provide sensitive information unless you trust the company and understand why it is needed.
  • Read payout requirements before spending time on an offer.
  • Keep a separate email address for reward and survey programs.
  • Leave immediately if a site pressures you to pay or promises guaranteed earnings.

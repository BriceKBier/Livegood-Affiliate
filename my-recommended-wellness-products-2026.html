What a COA means: These documents report testing for a particular product or lot. They help with transparency, but they do not prove that a supplement prevents, treats or cures a medical condition. Always check the current report and speak with a healthcare professional when appropriate.

Super Greens

I use a greens powder as a convenient addition to my routine. It helps on days when my diet is less than perfect, although I do not consider it a replacement for healthy food.

Ultra Magnesium Complex

Magnesium is involved in normal muscle, nerve and energy functions. I use a multi-form magnesium product, while keeping in mind that supplements should be discussed with a healthcare professional.

Organic Coffee

An organic coffee option is worth comparing if coffee is already part of your morning. Taste, price and how it fits your health situation matter more than hype.

My wellness philosophy

No single product creates good health. For me, wellness means eating reasonably well, staying active, sleeping, working with my doctors and using supplements only when they fit my situation.

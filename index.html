LiveGood: Wellness + Opportunity

LiveGood is a health and wellness company rooted in the belief that true transformation comes from “products + people.” Our mission: make premium, clean-label supplements and wellness goods accessible (without extreme markups), and give everyday people the opportunity to build income by sharing those products with others.

What LiveGood Offers

  • A full suite of products across wellness, fitness, detox, beauty, children’s health, pet health, and more.
  • Manufacturing in the U.S., under cGMP / NSF standards, with third-party testing and Certificates of Analysis (COAs).
  • A membership system—just $9.95/month or $99.95/year—that unlocks wholesale pricing.
  • A 90-day empty bottle guarantee for refunds.
  • Shipping in the U.S. (5–7 business days) and internationally (2–3 weeks).

The Business Opportunity (How You Can Earn)

  1. Become Affiliate + Member — lock in your position in a 2×15 matrix and get access to a business center.
  2. Weekly Fast Start Commissions — earn $25 per referral, plus bonus commissions up to 10 levels.
  3. Matrix Commissions — earn monthly based on the volume in your matrix.
  4. Matching Bonuses — match a percentage of your personally enrolled affiliates’ matrix earnings.
  5. Retail Commissions — earn 50% of the difference between retail and member pricing.
  6. Customer Acquisition Bonuses — 10% of a new member’s first product order (up to $300).
  7. Influencer Bonuses & Bonus Pools — extra commission at higher volumes and ranks.

Why This Might Be For You

  • Low risk, low entry — no inventory required.
  • Recurring revenue potential via memberships.
  • Multiple ways to earn: retail, recruitment, or both.
  • Leverage through team growth and matching bonuses.
  • Immediate benefit — wholesale savings even if you don’t build a business.

What You Should Be Honest About

  • Big income claims are based on ideal scenarios.
  • Recruiting often drives early growth more than retail.
  • Your income depends on members staying active.
  • Be careful with product and earnings claims (regulatory rules apply).
  • Consistency and effort matter for long-term results.
